Nightmare Realm is proud to present
Project Jingle Hell
A brand new haunt experience at Nightmare Realm for one night only...

November 5th, 2022 (7:30pm-11pm)
‍
Santa has found the town of Cypress Hollow to be very NAUGHTY this year, and has brought Krampus and his HELLves to fix this problem. A battle of the seasons is about to ensue while Santa is bringing the AXE back in X-MAS and taking the HALLOWeen out of Cypress HOLLOW.
‍
With the purchase of the General Admission ticket for ($25) our guests receive access to the Nightmare Realm presents Project Jingle Hell show, and re-entry for up to 2 more times during the same visit. This allows our guests a better view at our detailed sets to see things they may have missed prior.
‍
We will also be offering cold season favorites, like hot chocolate and an assortment of different cookies at our concession stand on the midway.
‍
Nightmare Realm is also lucky to have special guest appearances from Lily the Clown and Cid Claus.
