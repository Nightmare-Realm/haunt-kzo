Parking

Halloween and Seasonal Events

Nightmare Realm is a place of secrecy. We ask that all of our visitors park at the Paw Paw High School parking lot, which can be found at 30609 Red Arrow Hwy, Paw Paw, MI 49079. From there we will bus you over, free of charge, to the Nightmare Realm Terror Park. Your ride will be enhanced by the presence of one of our creatures, who will begin your journey into the mad world of retribution.

When we are not celebrating the haunt season, or celebrating a holiday haunting, please feel free to visit us at 23492 Red Arrow HWY, Mattawan MI 49071 for any special events.
